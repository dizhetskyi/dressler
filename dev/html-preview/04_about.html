Management Team

Christopher Dressler

Founder and President - Dressler Consulting LLC.

Chris provides clients with strategic business planning and integration services with an emphasis on infrastructure, data communications, and internet-related technologies. He has designed and developed enterprise e-business solutions and keystone applications.

Prior to Starting Dressler Consulting LLC., Chris was the Vice President of Bertelsmann Media Systems (BMS), the technical branch of Bertelsmann, the third largest media company in the world. Bertelsmann creates books, music, television and radio, magazines and newspapers, and Internet services. Bertelsmann provides services for the media with print, distribution and information technology. They also distribute media through clubs and online shops ï¿½ in 56 countries. Bertelsmann employs 82,000 people. During the last business year, they generated sales of more than 20 billion Euros. Bertelsmann is headquartered in Gutersloh, Germany.

Chris has worked in Europe for more than eight years with executive positions at three of the top five German media groups. He has over 15 years of IT and management experience. Chris began his career as a programmer analyst at Moog Automotive in St. Louis, Missouri. Seeking international experience, he moved to Frankfurt, Germany, in 1991, to work as a network management software developer. Chris also worked in Luxembourg to develop and build an e-commerce business for the Holzbrink Group of Saarbruken. Chris returned to the United States to build a new document management and workflow product group with Siemens.

He is a graduate of the University of Missouri-Columbia, and studied economics and computer science as an exchange student at the University of London, Imperial College.

Chris lives in Merrick, New York, with his wife Kathy, and their daughters Anja, Katya and Christa. He enjoys living on Long Island with the excitement of the city and the serenity of the ocean. In his free time, Chris loves to fish, sail, and a good game of golf.


Mark McGowan

Managing Partner - Dressler Consulting LLC.

Mark McGowan joined Dressler Consulting LLC in 2006 with more than twenty years of experience in IT, telecommunications and management challenges including ten years in senior management roles. He has designed, developed and implemented business solutions for a wide variety of clients.

Mark’s role of Partner includes advising clients on strategic business planning, integration services and program management. He has designed and developed web properties for startups and fortune 100 companies, designed commercial and government enterprise architectures, setup and run Project Management Offices (PMO) and implemented new technologies.

Prior to joining Dressler Consulting, Mark was the Managing Director of Technology and CTO of NOBOK Sports, an international media company offering sports fans unrivaled access and insight into legendary sports heroes and sports history. His role was to design, build and support the infrastructure and applications necessary to support the business. He also ran the PMO and developed revenue generation projections for the Board of Trusties.

He is particularly knowledgeable on the alignment of IT technologies to serve business aims with emphasis on improving the communication and use of information in business processes. He has worked in almost all major industry sectors on strategic initiatives to deliver business advantage from technology.

An experienced senior executive with strong skills in strategic, tactical and management aspects of technology, and service provision, Mark has been the founder or co-founder, of two technology companies that have been acquired by leading multinational technology companies.

Mark has an undergraduate degree from the University of Vermont. He holds an MBA degree from the George Washington University and a MS in the Management of Information Technology from the University of Virginia. He also has a Masters Certificate in Project Management from the George Washington University.

Mark lives in Pelham, New York, with his wife and three children. He coaches lacrosse, soccer and ice hockey and enjoys fishing, boating, golfing and organized sports.
